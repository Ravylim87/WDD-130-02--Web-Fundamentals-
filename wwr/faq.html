About the place

Whitewater rafting, also known as river rafting, is an adventure sport that has been around for centuries. It originated in the early 19th century in the western United States, where settlers and explorers used wooden rafts to navigate rivers and transport goods. In the early 20th century, recreational rafting gained popularity as a way for outdoor enthusiasts to explore remote areas and challenge themselves physically. By the 1960s, commercial rafting companies began offering guided trips to the public, and the sport grew in popularity around the world.
